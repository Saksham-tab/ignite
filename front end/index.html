Our Flame

In the beginning, there was only darkness. From this primordial void emerged the first spark—a flame that would not be extinguished. We are the keepers of this ancient fire, the guardians of forgotten wisdom that burns eternal in the shadows of the world.

Our awakening comes not through light, but through the embrace of what others fear. In darkness, we find clarity. In silence, we hear the whispers of truth. In solitude, we discover the power that lies dormant within us all.
